Mee Goreng Recipe

Ingredients of Mee Goreng

  • 2 tbsp canola oil
  • 300g smoked tofu, diced
  • â…› cabbage, shredded
  • 1-2 carrots, cut into matchsticks
  • 1 green capsicum, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp chilli sauce
  • 2 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp tomato ketchup
  • 2 tbsp dark sweet soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp peanut oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, peeled and finely chopped
  • 2 shallots, peeled and finely sliced
  • 2 chicken breasts, skinless and boneless, finely sliced
  • 1 potato, peeled, boiled and diced
  • 2 tsp curry powder
  • 50gm beansprouts
  • 500gm fresh cooked yellow noodles (mee)
  • 2 squid, cleaned and cut into rings

Steps to make

  1. Boil the yellow noodles until al dente, then drain and set aside.
  2. Heat the canola oil in a wok over high heat, then add the smoked tofu and stir-fry until golden.
  3. Add the garlic, shallots, and chicken, and stir-fry until the chicken is cooked through.
  4. Push the ingredients to the side of the wok, then add the beaten egg and scramble until set.
  5. Stir in the cabbage, carrots, and green capsicum, and cook until the vegetables are slightly softened.
  6. Add the chili sauce, oyster sauce, tomato ketchup, and dark sweet soy sauce, and stir to combine.
  7. Toss in the boiled noodles, curry powder, and beansprouts, and stir-fry until well combined and heated through.
  8. Garnish with boiled potato, squid rings, and serve hot.
